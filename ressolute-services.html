✦ Our Overview

We offer three levels of support

Clarity, Deep-Dive, and Pathway — each building on the level before, depending on the depth and continuity your situation requires.



Step 1

Many clients begin with a Clarity session to make sense of things. From there, work can naturally develop into a more detailed Deep-Dive or ongoing Pathway support as the situation unfolds.

Step 2

Where additional depth or time is required, this is always discussed and agreed in advance, ensuring a transparent and considered approach throughout.

Step 3

This allows us to stay responsive to what your situation actually requires, rather than forcing it into a fixed structure.

Level 1: Clarity

A calm, structured space to make sense of things

When things feel unclear, overwhelming, or difficult to navigate, this is the place to start.

Includes:

  • A focused 60–90 minute session
  • Careful unpacking of your situation
  • Identification of key patterns and priorities
  • Clear immediate next steps

You leave with:

  • a clearer understanding of what's going on
  • direction you can act on
Guide Price: £120 – £180

Level 2: Deep Dive

When you need more than answers — you need understanding

The Deep-Dive includes everything in Clarity, followed by focused research and structured exploration of your situation.

Includes:

  • A Clarity session to fully understand your situation
  • Focused research into key areas
  • Full situation mapping
  • Identification of options and pathways
  • A clear, structured breakdown of findings

You leave with:

  • a grounded understanding of your situation
  • informed, realistic options
  • a clear way forward
Guide Price: £300 – £750
(depending on scope and complexity)

Level 3: Pathway

Ongoing support for situations that evolve over time, includes clarity and deep dive

Pathway includes both Clarity and the full Deep-Dive process, with continued support as your situation develops.

Includes:

  • A Clarity session and full Deep-Dive process
  • Ongoing thinking and support
  • Evolving research and refinement
  • Decision support as new information arises
  • Regular check-ins

You leave with:

  • continuity and stability
  • clarity across changing circumstances
  • support in moving forward with confidence
From £400 – £1,200+ per month

(depending on level of involvement)

Ongoing Partnership

For situations requiring longer-term continuity, ongoing partnership options are available.

For when your situation continues to evolve

Some situations don't resolve in a single phase — they unfold over time. For these, we offer ongoing partnership options, allowing us to stay close to your situation, respond as things develop, and continue refining the path forward.

What this means:

  • continuity of understanding
  • no need to re-explain your situation
  • responsive support when things change
  • deeper, more informed decision-making over time

How it works:

Ongoing support is tailored to the level of involvement required and is usually arranged following a Deep-Dive or Pathway phase.
