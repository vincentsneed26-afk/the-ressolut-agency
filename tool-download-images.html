ResSolut — Download & Rename Images

How to use this tool

1
Click Download All Images below — each image will save to your Downloads folder with a descriptive filename.
2
Move the downloaded files into your Website/images/ folder.
3
Then open tool-fix-url-paths.html to update your HTML files to point to the local images.
⚠️ Note — if downloads don't start automatically: Your browser may block multiple downloads at once. If prompted, click "Allow" to permit downloads from this page. Or use the individual download buttons on each image card.
